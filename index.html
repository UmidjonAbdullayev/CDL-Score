The flag system
Instant hiring signal at a glance

Every driver in our database receives one of three flags based on their verified history. No guesswork — just a clear signal backed by real carrier data.

Green — Safe to hire
Strong employment history, passed all drug tests, positive reviews from multiple carriers. This driver has a proven track record of reliability and professionalism.
Verify first
Some concerns on record — gaps in employment, a single incident, or mixed carrier feedback. Review the full profile and conduct your own due diligence before proceeding.
Red — Do not hire
Serious red events on file — failed drug test, truck abandonment, DOT violations, or multiple terminations for cause. Hiring this driver puts your carrier at serious risk.
